Adding your links

You can add an easy access to your favorite websites in the Quick Links settings. Just type your site's title, its URL and click Add, it will appear under the weather widget. If you want to get rid of a link, just press it for a second and the delete icon will appear.

Weather

Bonjourr's weather defaults to Paris. Change it by typing in the name of your city and click Change city, or select Automatic to set the weather based on your geolocation. You can also change the measure unit.
