Adding your quick links

You can add an easy access to your favorite websites in the Quick Links settings. Just type your site's title, its URL and click Add, it will appear under the weather widget.

Hold your mouse for a second on any link to enter remove mode.

Weather

By default, Bonjourr's weather is taken from Paris. Type in the name of the city you want the weather of and click Change city

With your permission, Bonjourr can setup the weather based on your location.

Backgrounds

There are three ways of choosing a background in Bonjourr.

The Choose background option lets your choose from our default selection.

The Dynamic background option will change your background automatically every day and picks them randomly from a regularly updated selection.

And thirdly, the Custom Background option will let your choose an image from your local computer.

Search bar

If you want, you can activate a search bar.

You can choose your favorite search engine from a wide variety.
